Biography

Hello! This is Isidro Jara.

I am a Data Engineer and Data Warehouse Expert currently working at Idorsia Pharmaceuticals Ltd in Allschwil, Switzerland. My role involves developing and maintaining data reports and other visualizations, assisting teams in uncovering valuable insights, and integrating data to support our analytics platform.

Previously, I worked at Hoffmann-La Roche where I gained experience in Data Science and Business Analysis. I have also worked at NTT Data and Sky Italia, where I focused on Data Architecture and ETL dataflows.

Always willing to learn and overcome new academic challenges, I hold a Master's in Artificial Intelligence (@UNIR), a Master's in Cybersecurity and Information Security (@UCLM), and a Master's in Telecommunications Engineering and Degree in Technology and Telecommunication and Services Engineering (both @UPM).

I have recently started my PhD studies in Machine Learning for Complex Medical Cases at UNIR. I am also studying a Degree in Mathematics and Degree in Philosophy (both @UNED).
